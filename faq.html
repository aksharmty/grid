Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Sakhiraj Grid Bot, subscriptions and platform usage.

What is Sakhiraj Grid Bot?

Sakhiraj Grid Bot is a cloud-based trading platform for KuCoin Spot Market. It follows a predefined Grid + DCA strategy with automatic profit booking, Global Exit and compounding.

Do I need to download any software?

No. The platform runs entirely on the cloud. Simply log in through your web browser and manage your trading account online.

Which exchange is supported?

Currently, Sakhiraj Grid Bot supports KuCoin Spot Trading.

Can I change the trading strategy?

No. Every subscriber uses the same predefined trading strategy. This helps keep the platform simple, consistent and easy to use.

Does the bot support Futures Trading?

No. The platform is designed only for KuCoin Spot Trading.

How does the bot buy coins?

The platform follows fixed trading rules. Additional buy orders are placed when market conditions match the predefined Grid + DCA strategy.

What is Global Exit?

The platform continuously monitors the combined value of all active holdings. When the predefined overall profit target is reached, all active positions may be closed together to release capital for the next trading cycle.

Does the platform use compounding?

Yes. When possible, the platform attempts to increase future order sizes as trading capital grows.

Is my API secure?

Users connect their KuCoin account using API credentials. Trading permissions are required, but withdrawal permission should always remain disabled for security.

How long is the subscription valid?

Each subscription is valid for one year from the activation date.

Will I always make a profit?

No. Cryptocurrency markets are volatile. No trading platform or strategy can guarantee profits.

Can I use multiple trading pairs?

The available trading pairs depend on the platform features and your subscription.

How can I access my account?

Subscribers can securely log in through the browser and monitor trading history, holdings and account activity online.

Can I stop the bot anytime?

Yes. You can request to stop your bot at any time. However, stopping the bot does not pause or extend your subscription. Your subscription remains valid until its original expiry date.

What happens when my subscription expires?

When your subscription expires, the bot stops placing new buy and sell orders. Your trading history and account records remain available for up to 30 days. After that period, data may be removed from the platform according to our data retention policy.

Can I renew my subscription before it expires?

Yes. You can renew your annual subscription before the expiry date to continue using the platform without interruption.

Can I withdraw my funds anytime?

Yes. Your funds always remain in your own KuCoin account, so you can withdraw them at any time according to KuCoin's policies and available balance. Sakhiraj Grid Bot never holds, transfers, or controls your funds. The platform only places buy and sell orders through the API that you authorize.

How do I get support?

Support is available for subscription, platform usage and general account-related questions through our official contact channels.
