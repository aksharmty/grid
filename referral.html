Commission Policy

Referral commission is credited only for eligible subscription purchases completed through your referral link.

  • Current referral commission rate: 10% of the eligible subscription amount.
  • Commission is added to your Referral Account 45 days after a successful purchase.
  • Cancelled, refunded or disputed orders are not eligible for commission.
  • Commission is credited only after the order successfully completes the verification period.
  • Referral commission percentage may be changed, increased or decreased at any time without prior notice.

Withdrawal Policy

  • Minimum withdrawal: ₹1,000
  • Withdrawal requests can be submitted once per calendar month.
