Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sakhiraj Grid Bot collects, uses and protects information when you use our cloud-based trading platform.

Information We Collect

  • Name (if provided)
  • Email Address
  • KuCoin UID
  • Trading API Information
  • Subscription Details
  • Login Records
  • Trading History

How We Use Your Information

  • Activate your subscription.
  • Provide cloud trading services.
  • Connect your KuCoin account.
  • Display trading history.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Improve platform performance.

API Security

Users connect their KuCoin account using API credentials. We strongly recommend creating an API with trading permission only. Withdrawal permission should always remain disabled.

Sakhiraj Grid Bot never requests your KuCoin account password.

Your Funds

Your cryptocurrency and funds always remain in your personal KuCoin account.

Sakhiraj Grid Bot does not hold, receive, transfer or withdraw customer funds.

Data Retention

Trading history and account information are retained while your subscription is active.

After subscription expiry, your account history may remain available for up to 30 days before being removed from our systems.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve login sessions, website functionality and user experience.

Third-Party Services

Our website may contain services provided by third parties including KuCoin, Google Analytics, Google AdSense and advertising partners. These services operate according to their own privacy policies.

Data Protection

We implement reasonable security measures to protect customer information. However, no online system can guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

  • Request correction of your account information.
  • Contact us regarding your subscription.
  • Request account removal where applicable.

Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
