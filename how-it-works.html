How Sakhiraj Grid Bot Works

Sakhiraj Grid Bot is a cloud-based trading platform designed around a single, fixed trading strategy. Instead of providing dozens of settings and indicators, the platform follows predefined rules that focus on disciplined execution, capital management and continuous trading.

One Strategy. No Confusion.

Many trading bots provide dozens of settings that often confuse users and lead to inconsistent results. Sakhiraj Grid Bot follows one carefully designed strategy. Every subscriber uses the same trading logic, making the platform simple, consistent and easy to understand.

Step 1 - Automatic Buy

When the market price falls by approximately 3% from the previous buy level, the bot automatically places a new buy order.

This creates a Grid + DCA structure without requiring manual intervention.

Step 2 - Regular Profit Booking

Each individual buy order targets approximately 4% profit. Whenever that target is achieved, the corresponding order is sold automatically while the remaining Grid continues running.

Step 3 - Global Exit

Sometimes several buy orders remain open together. Instead of waiting for every order to reach its individual target, the bot continuously calculates the combined value of all active positions.

Whenever the overall portfolio reaches approximately 3% net profit, the bot exits all remaining positions together and releases the capital for a fresh trading cycle.

Designed with Indian Traders in Mind

The trading logic has been developed with practical capital management in mind. The Global Exit mechanism helps free locked funds so that trading can continue instead of leaving large balances idle for long periods.

The strategy has also been designed keeping the impact of India's crypto TDS system in mind, with the objective of maintaining continuous trading while managing available capital efficiently.

Automatic Compounding

As account balance grows, the platform attempts to increase the order size gradually. This allows profits to participate in future trades without requiring manual adjustments.
