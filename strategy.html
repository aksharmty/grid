Bot Trading Strategy

Sakhiraj Grid Bot follows a predefined Grid + DCA + Compounding strategy for KuCoin Spot Trading. Every order is executed automatically according to fixed trading rules. No indicators or manual strategy selection are required.

1. Initial Buy

When the bot starts, it places the first Market Buy.

  • 0.5% of your available KuCoin trading capital.
  • Minimum order size is 1 USDT.
  • The higher value between these two is used.

2. Grid Buying

After the first purchase, the bot continuously monitors the market.

  • A new Grid Buy is placed every 1.5% price drop from the previous buy.
  • Each new buy amount increases automatically by approximately 0.1% to 0.2%.
  • This gradual increase provides automatic compounding over time.

3. Automatic DCA Buy

Markets do not always fall by exactly 1.5%. During sideways movement, no new Grid Buy may occur.

If no Grid Buy is executed for 20 consecutive hours, the bot automatically places one additional DCA Buy at the current market price.

  • Maintains trading activity.
  • Improves average entry price.
  • Keeps capital deployment active.

4. Individual Sell

Each buy order maintains its own profit target.

  • The most recent buy (Last Buy Row) is sold first.
  • Sell orders follow the regular Take Profit settings configured for the bot.
  • Earlier Grid Buy orders remain active until their conditions are met.

5. Global Exit

Along with regular individual sells, the bot continuously evaluates the combined performance of all active positions.

When the configured Global Target is reached, all remaining active buy orders are sold together according to your Global Exit settings.

  • Releases trading capital.
  • Completes one trading cycle.
  • Allows the next cycle to begin with fresh capital.
