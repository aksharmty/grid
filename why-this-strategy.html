Why This Strategy?

Sakhiraj Grid Bot was not designed to offer dozens of trading strategies or hundreds of settings. It was built around one carefully tested trading approach with a simple objective: keep trading disciplined, manage capital efficiently, and avoid unnecessary complexity.

Instead of asking users to choose indicators, configure technical parameters, or constantly adjust settings, the platform follows a predefined strategy that executes automatically according to fixed rules.

Why We Don't Offer Multiple Strategies

Many trading platforms allow users to select different indicators, signals, and strategy combinations. While flexibility may sound attractive, it also increases complexity and often leads to inconsistent trading decisions.

Sakhiraj Grid Bot follows a different philosophy. Every subscriber uses the same trading logic, allowing the platform to remain simple, predictable, and easy to operate.

Designed for Long-Term Trading

The objective is not to chase every market movement. Instead, the strategy focuses on maintaining continuous trading activity while managing available capital responsibly.

The bot combines Grid Trading and DCA into a single workflow, allowing positions to be built gradually during price declines while continuing to book profits whenever market conditions permit.

Why Global Exit Matters

Traditional Grid Trading may leave several buy orders waiting for long periods. As more capital becomes locked in open positions, traders often find it difficult to continue trading efficiently.

Sakhiraj Grid Bot continuously monitors the combined value of all active holdings. Whenever the overall portfolio reaches approximately 3% profit, the platform exits all remaining positions together and prepares the account for a fresh trading cycle.

This approach is designed to reduce long-term capital blockage and keep funds available for future opportunities.

Capital Management First

Profit is only one part of successful trading. Efficient use of capital is equally important. The strategy attempts to recycle available funds whenever possible instead of allowing large balances to remain inactive for extended periods.

Built with Indian Traders in Mind

India's crypto taxation system presents unique challenges for active traders. The trading logic has been designed with these practical considerations in mind, including the impact of TDS on available trading capital.

The objective is to support continuous trading while helping users manage their available balance more efficiently.

Automatic Compounding

As trading capital grows, the platform attempts to increase future order sizes automatically. This allows profits to participate in subsequent trading cycles without requiring manual adjustments.
