Capital Management

Successful trading is not only about making profitable trades. It is also about using available trading capital efficiently. Sakhiraj Grid Bot has been designed to keep capital working whenever practical, rather than allowing large portions of the account to remain inactive for extended periods.

Beyond Traditional Grid Trading

In a traditional Grid strategy, several buy orders may remain open while waiting for individual profit targets. During prolonged market declines, this can gradually reduce the amount of capital available for new opportunities.

Sakhiraj Grid Bot combines Grid Trading with DCA and continuous portfolio monitoring to help maintain an active trading cycle.

Global Exit Concept

Instead of evaluating every position independently, the platform continuously monitors the combined value of all active holdings.

When the total portfolio reaches the predefined overall profit target, the bot closes all active positions together and prepares the account for a fresh trading cycle.

This approach is intended to release trading capital so that new opportunities can be captured without waiting for every individual position to reach its separate target.

Continuous Trading Cycle

  • Price declines trigger additional buy orders according to the predefined strategy.
  • Individual positions continue targeting regular profits.
  • The platform monitors overall portfolio performance.
  • Global Exit may close all remaining positions when the overall target is achieved.
  • A new trading cycle begins using the available capital.

Automatic Compounding

As trading capital increases over time, Sakhiraj Grid Bot attempts to increase future order sizes automatically wherever possible.

This allows accumulated profits to participate in future trading cycles without requiring manual adjustments from the user.

Designed with Practical Trading in Mind

The platform has been designed with practical trading considerations in mind, including efficient capital utilization and the impact that frequent spot trading can have on available account balance over time.

The objective is to support uninterrupted trading activity while helping users make effective use of their available funds.
