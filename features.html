Platform Features

Sakhiraj Grid Bot is designed to provide a simple, reliable and cloud-based trading experience. Every feature is built to reduce complexity while helping traders manage their KuCoin Spot Trading efficiently.

Cloud-Based Platform

The bot runs entirely on the cloud. There is no software to install, no VPS to manage and no updates to download.

Browser-Based Access

Access your trading dashboard securely from any modern web browser. Simply log in to monitor your account and trading history.

KuCoin Spot Trading

The platform is built exclusively for KuCoin Spot Trading and does not support Futures Trading.

Fixed Grid + DCA Strategy

Every subscriber uses the same predefined trading strategy. There are no complicated settings or strategy selection options, making the platform simple and consistent.

Global Exit System

Instead of evaluating only individual trades, the platform monitors the overall portfolio and can release capital when predefined portfolio profit conditions are met.

Automatic Compounding

Whenever possible, the platform attempts to increase order values as trading capital grows, helping improve long-term capital utilization.

Smart Capital Management

The strategy is designed to reduce unnecessary capital lock by combining Grid trading with portfolio-based profit management.

Buy & Sell History

Users can log in through their browser and review completed trades, active positions and account history.

Secure API Integration

The platform connects to your KuCoin account using Trading API permissions only. Withdrawal permission should always remain disabled for security.

Annual Subscription

Sakhiraj Grid Bot is available through a simple annual subscription model with regular platform improvements and customer support.
