About Sakhiraj Grid Bot

Sakhiraj Grid Bot is a cloud-based trading platform developed to simplify KuCoin Spot Grid Trading for traders who want automation without installing software. Our goal is to provide a reliable, secure, and easy-to-use solution that allows users to manage their trading directly from a web browser.

Our mission is to make automated crypto trading accessible to everyone. By offering a cloud platform with a simple interface, users can focus on their trading strategy instead of technical setup or software maintenance.

Technology

Sakhiraj Grid Bot is a browser-based cloud platform. Users simply log in to their account, connect their KuCoin API securely, and monitor trading activity, order history, and account performance online. There is no software to download or install.
